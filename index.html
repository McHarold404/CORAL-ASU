Complex Data Reasoning and Analysis (CoRAL) Lab at Arizona State University


Directed by: Dr. Vivek Gupta

At the CoRAL Lab, we are at the forefront of integrating cutting-edge AI technologies across natural language processing, computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to develop advanced AI systems that excel in reasoning, controllability, and reliability, with a particular focus on navigating complex and structured data.

Our Focus:

  • Human-Centric Innovations: We leverage insights from human reasoning to craft techniques that adeptly handle a variety of data types, including tables, charts, and graphs. Our goal is to enhance AI's ability to understand and interact with complex datasets in ways that mirror human cognitive processes.
  • Advanced Technologies: Utilizing the latest advancements in Large Language Models (LLMs) and Visual Language Models (VLMs), we push the boundaries of AIâ€™s capabilities in interpreting and reasoning about text, spreadsheets, and multimedia content. This helps us build more robust and versatile AI systems.
  • Real-World Impact: Our research addresses a wide range of applications, from question-answering and inference to structure generation and neural-symbolic techniques. We focus on practical solutions for data extraction, intelligent document processing, and sophisticated data visualization, with applications spanning healthcare, finance, sports, e-commerce, and education.

Our Mission:

We are committed to revolutionizing industries by creating AI systems that enhance decision-making, automate complex processes, and ensure transparency and reliability. Our work drives meaningful improvements across a variety of sectors. We prioritize ethical, interpretable AI systems and strive for collaborations with academic institutions and industry partners to address real-world challenges and advance the field of AI.
